Lost Academy Retired

Lost Academy officially retired as an active Jedi Academy clan on July 5, 2026.

Thank y'all to every member, ally, rival, and friend who helped shape the community, memories, and legacy of Lost Academy.

This website will remain online as a historical archive until the domain expires.

About Lost Academy

Lost Academy was a Jedi Academy community focused on respect, friendship, events, and a structured rank path. During its active years, LA hosted public and private servers, training sessions, tournaments, seasonal competitions, and countless nights spent playing together.

Founded on January 15, 2024, Lost Academy officially retired as an active Jedi Academy clan on July 5, 2026. This website remains online to preserve the history, memories, and legacy of the community.
